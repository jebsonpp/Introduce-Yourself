My name is Jeb, and I am a third-year CS student. Ever since I was young, I’ve had a passion for collecting things—whether it’s memorabilia, unique trinkets, or anything that catches my eye. I find joy in acquiring and organizing my collections, each item holding a story or memory that makes it special. Apart from collecting, I also enjoy spending money—sometimes impulsively, sometimes with purpose. Whether it’s on hobbies, experiences, or things that make life a little more interesting, I believe in making the most out of what I have. One of my biggest interests is video games, especially hardcore survival games. Titles like Valheim and Project Zomboid captivate me because of their challenging mechanics, deep survival elements, and the thrill of overcoming obstacles in a harsh, immersive world. These games test patience, strategy, and resilience—skills that I admire and try to develop in real life as well. As I continue my journey as a student and gamer, I look forward to expanding my experiences, growing my collections, and finding new ways to enjoy life.
